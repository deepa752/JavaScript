Welcome to Hangman

    How to play?

  • Based on the level you choose, you will be provided with a limited number of lives.
  • Easy : 9 Lives
  • Medium : 6 Lives
  • Hard : 3 Lives
  • You have to guess a letter of the word chosen by the computer.
  • If your guess is correct then the blank corresponding to the guessed letter will be filled.
  • If your guess is not correct your lives are reduced by 1.
  • If you have no lives left you lose.
  • If you are able to guess the complete word before running out of lives then you win.
